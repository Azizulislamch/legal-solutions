Frequently Asked Any Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our legal services, consultation process, and how we can help you navigate your legal journey.

1. Are application fee waivers available?

—

Yes, we offer various financial assistance options and fee waivers depending on the case type and individual circumstances. Please contact our office for detailed eligibility criteria.

2. What documents are needed for consultation?

+

Please bring any relevant legal notices, identity documents, and past case records if available. This helps our team understand your situation more accurately from the start.

+

The initial process usually takes about 3 to 5 business days, including case review and initial scheduling. We prioritize urgent matters to ensure timely legal support.
